ğŸŒ SEO & Google Search Console Setup
To ensure Google recommends your news, follow these steps:
Sitemap: Upload sitemap.xml to your root directory.
Publisher Center: Submit your domain to the Google News Publisher Center.
Search Console: Verify your property and submit the sitemap.xml URL.
Tracking: All traffic is monitored via the integrated Google Tag Manager ID: G-8NEMWLX7BF.
